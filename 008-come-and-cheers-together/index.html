008 Come and cheers together

2023-09-09 2 min read # speaker # black






I love my desk, especially the black speaker on the right. I usually use it when listening to music and studying. It is the Yiyin White Noise Speaker Pro. Because it has more than 10 types of ambient white noise built in, and I play it when studying, so I say it can be used for studying.

It is not a night light. The pattern on it is the moon. As time goes by in the lunar calendar, the moon will also show the pattern of waxing and waning. Just like today is the 21st of the seventh lunar month, not long after the full moon, it is already the last quarter of the moon.

I didn’t put a socket on the desktop, so when the battery ran out, I put it on the speaker for wireless charging. Although wireless is not faster than wired, it gives me a reason to spend more time studying instead of wasting time when my phone is full immediately.
