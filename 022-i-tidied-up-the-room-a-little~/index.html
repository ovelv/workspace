022 I tidied up the room a little~

2023-09-12 2 min read # sharemydesk # desktopmakeover











Sure enough, the most comfortable time every day is sitting in front of my small desk on a sunny afternoon. Whether it is reading a book, watching a movie, or just relaxing in a daze, it is very comfortable.

What I’m very happy about recently is that a friend gave me a small Bluetooth speaker with a very special shape that looks like a scorpion. As a boy who loves electronic products, I really like this small Gravity Planet speaker.

After using it for a few days, I feel that in addition to its pretty good sound quality (especially the strong low-accent sound, suitable for watching movies and playing games), it also looks very attractive. As its name suggests, it also has a handle like a stinger, making it very easy to move.

There is an RGB ambient light on it that can be adjusted at will. Besides listening to music, it is also quite suitable to be placed on the table as an exquisite little ornament or a small ambient light.

It is one of the few electronic products that I think is easy to use, looks good and is fun to play.
