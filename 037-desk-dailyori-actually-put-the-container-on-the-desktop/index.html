037 Desk Daily｜I actually put the container on the desktop⁉️

2023-09-21 2 min read # Desktop Sharing






As there are more and more electronic products on the desktop, more and more data lines are circling around the desktop.
I just happened to see a high-looking desktop power strip (gallium nitride fast charging power strip), which breaks the traditional charger design and is the first time I have seen a charger designed in a container shape~

Let me share my experience of using it below~

✅Good appearance, good table setting
This power strip has a cool industrial style, with a square package and a three-port smiley face design. I didn’t expect that even the power strip could be so high-end. The red color also goes well with my white desktop.

✅Small and exquisite, portable for travel
This small container can be said to be compact and exquisite. It is easy to store on the desktop and does not take up space. It is very easy to carry when going to the library for business trips and office work.

✅65w fast charging, five ports in one
2 Type-c sockets + 1 USB socket + 1 five-flat hole, it is both a power strip and a charger. There is no need for multiple charging heads to be plugged together in a mess. The most important thing is that the Type-c socket supports With 65w output, it charges the phone really fast.

If you like the baby, you can give it a try~
