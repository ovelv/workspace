🧑🏻‍💻As a designer and blogger, it’s normal to stay up late and work on pictures. After sitting for a long time, I feel most sorry for my cervical and lumbar spine. A good chair is a strong support for my long battery life.

The Xihao Doro C300 cloud white ergonomic chair that I recently bought fits very well with the light-colored desk style that I like recently. This white is a very high-end stain-resistant white with a bit of gray tones, and it looks very good. It’s my favorite. Love the fresh style. Paired with cloud mesh, it is breathable and comfortable without being stuffy.

📌The adaptive lumbar support is really friendly to workers. It can fit tightly against the waist whether sitting or lying down, and can make corresponding changes in strength and position without manual adjustment. With the large recline angle of 138 degrees, you can lie back when you are tired from work. After all, you can continue to work only after you have rested, right?

📌The 6D's bionic joint armrests are very flexible and can adapt to my working status at any time. When I want to raise my hands to play games after sitting for a long time, they will also provide effective support according to my movements. It is a great entertainment and office experience.

📌The portable one-button control system rejects cumbersome button settings and integrates the lift adjustment/sliding seat adjustment/tilt adjustment knobs into one. It creates an operation logic based on everyone's application habits and makes adjustments quickly and in one step.

💡This is where the chair can help. Sitting for long periods of time is really harmful to the cervical and lumbar spine. In the end, you still have to remember to get up and walk around more, drink water and move around. Making money sustainably is the creed of contemporary young people.
