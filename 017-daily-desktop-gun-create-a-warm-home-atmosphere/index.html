017 Daily Desktop丨🟠⚪Create a warm home atmosphere

2023-09-10 3 min read # warm # DesktopShare # desktop









🧑🏻‍💻The significance of desktop layout is not only to create an efficient workbench, but also to create a healing harbor where you can be alone, study and relax. After a busy day at work, I like to sit in my little corner late at night, because this moment only belongs to me.

✨Late at night, as the city lights go out, the lights in the small corners of my home light up, making me feel cozy and comfortable with all the things I love. The core of my corner lighting atmosphere is large-scale rendering and detailed embellishment. Enhance the visual experience while taking into account fun and personalization.

💡The main light source comes from BenQ’s Screenbar Halo, which fully illuminates the work area without causing screen reflection. The hanging installation method does not occupy desktop space, and the wireless controller can also make the desktop more tidy and beautiful.

💡The warm yellow ambient light on the back comes from Philips' Hue Play gradient light strip, which is fixed on the back of the monitor with 3M glue (the specific installation method can be viewed on my homepage). It is compatible with Homekit smart home, and you can use Siri to control switches and color changes.

💡The planted pine cone camp lamp is small and cute. It can be combined into different shapes with a cowhide lampshade. It has three levels of brightness and infinitely adjustable color temperature, which can easily create a warm atmosphere. The built-in lanyard also unlocks more usage scenarios, not only as a desktop ornament but also as a wear accessory~
