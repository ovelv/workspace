018 🟠⚪Warm white table setting｜Summer should be so bright and clear🍃

2023-09-11 3 min read # sharemydesktop










☀️Cold comes and summer comes, and an ideal life requires us to feel the cycle of the four seasons with our heart.

💻Although the study room is small, it is my favorite corner. I use a warm white table set to interpret my understanding of summer, where I can study, rest, and spend time alone.

🧾Equipment list:
⚪Ergonomic chair: Yongyi X3
⚪Height-lifting table: Lanliduo H2pro
⚪Monitor: Feiyi Lebu
⚪Screen hanging light: Depp 80cm
⚪Speaker: Edifier Huazai Abyss Mirror
⚪Keyboard: Mini Lo 75
⚪Mouse: Logitech GPW2
⚪Game console: XSS/Switch
⚪Game controller: G7se

🏷️My pure white desk and ergonomic chair are both liftable and can be adjusted to a height that suits you based on your height and sitting habits.

🏷️Yongyi X3 ergonomic chair is also equipped with magic armrests, which support large lifting and large-angle rotation. Whether sitting or lying down, the armrests can be flexibly adjusted to a comfortable position to support the main arm, making it very comfortable for working and playing games. When storing the chair, lower the armrests so it can be easily pushed under the tabletop to save space.

🏷️The chair can also adjust the headrest, backrest, lumbar support, seat depth and recline angle as needed, and the flocked chair surface is skin-friendly and breathable, with both resilience and support, making it less tiring to sit for long periods of time.

🏷️It’s perfectly OK to take an immersive lunch break in the summer afternoon. The hidden footrest can be used by pulling it out. It can be hidden and stored when not in use, so it doesn’t take up space. You don’t need to install it yourself, you can lay it flat once you get it out of the box.

🧑🏻‍💻Share my table setting and my attitude towards life.
Choose a comfortable state, let your body senses relax enough, feel it while working hard, and become a better version of yourself.
